What is MESA?

Introducing a new music streaming service that adapts to your emotional preferences based on various activities such as exercising, sleeping, studying, and more. Our service is equipped with machine learning algorithms that analyze your music choices and tailor personalized playlists to suit your mood and activity. Whether you need high-energy tunes for your morning workout or relaxing melodies for bedtime, our service has got you covered. Say goodbye to generic playlists and hello to a music streaming service that truly understands your unique preferences.

Why use MESA?

Music has been found to have a significant impact on human emotions and behavior. Research has shown that music can affect mood, motivation, and physiological responses such as heart rate and breathing rate[1]. This suggests that music can be a useful tool for achieving daily goals like exercising, sleeping, and focusing.

Studies have also found that listening to music during exercise can improve performance and reduce perceived exertion [2]. Similarly, listening to calming music before bed has been shown to improve sleep quality [3]. By providing personalized playlists that match users' emotional states and daily goals, our music streaming service could potentially enhance these benefits.
